# Summary of Russian Civil Code Articles

**Overview**
The provided text outlines key articles from the Russian Civil Code concerning the protection of civil rights, liability for damages, and the legal capacity of individuals.

**File: 5_grazhdanskijj_kodeks_rossijjskojj_federacii_chast_pervaya.pdf**

* Courts can invalidate unlawful acts by state or municipal bodies.
* Individuals may engage in proportional self-defense of their rights.
* Violated parties are entitled to full compensation for losses (damages and lost profits).
* The state must compensate for losses caused by its unlawful actions.
* All citizens possess equal civil legal capacity from birth until death.

**Conclusion**
These provisions establish a framework for judicial redress, self-help, financial compensation, and the fundamental legal standing of individuals under Russian civil law.
